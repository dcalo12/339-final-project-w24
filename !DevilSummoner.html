Devil Summoner

Series Description

The "Devil Summoner" series is a spin-off of ATLUS' "Shin Megami Tensei" series, which itself is an off-shoot of the "Megami Tensei" series. This marked the beginning of the ginormous web of subseries and spin-offs that this franchise is famous for. And the reason why I have to make a 50-page website for this final project...

This series is focused on people who have the ability to summon "devils" (commonly referred to as "demons" in all games), who are commonly called "Devil Summoners" (shocker), and their interactions in the world-ending situations common in the franchise.
