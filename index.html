Dante's Personal Megami Tensei Page

Welcome to my personal page about the Megami Tensei franchise, a collection of niche games (primarily JRPGs) I discovered a few years ago and have fallen in love with.

This will contain pages for multiple game series, such as "Shin Megami Tensei," "Persona" (ew), "Devil Summoner," and more, depending on how in-depth I choose to go. These pages will have a breif synopsis if I've played them, a link to a Wikipedia article where applicable, lots of pictures to look at, and cool statistics like sales and critic scores.

This is a HUGE franchise, so it wouldn't be productive to try and cover every single game it contains.

With that said, move to the navigation area above and choose a series to look at!
